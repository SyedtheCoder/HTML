Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of 11 players.
The objective is to score more runs than the opposing team while also dismissing their players.
The game is played in different formats, including Test matches (lasting up to five days), One Day Internationals (ODIs) (50 overs per team), and T20s (20 overs per team).
A match consists of batting, bowling, and fielding, with players aiming to score runs by hitting the ball and preventing the opposing team from scoring.
The sport is governed by the International Cricket Council (ICC) and follows specific rules and regulations.
